Swffy FAQ

Why not Flashpoint?

Flashpoint is most commonly used with a program/app, and I wanted to use it on web. Also, I'm pretty bored and wanted to do something cool.

Where are the games sourced?

For the first couple, I'll grab them from Flashpoint (love you flashpoint <3) and will link the source. For user uploads, I'll add a source field once everythings up and running.
